Brownie Fudge

Ingredients

  • 90 g (3 oz) dark chocolate, coarsely chopped
  • 100 g (3 1/2 oz) butter
  • 3/4 cup (150 g) sugar
  • 1/2 compact cup (100 g) brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs and 1 yolk, beaten together
  • 2/3 cup (100 g) wheat flour
  • 3 spoons cocoa powder

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a square 18 cm (7 in) mold. Line the base with baking parchment.
  2. Mix the chocolate and butter in a heatproof bowl. Place the bowl over a pan of simmering water and let it melt. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  3. Mix both sugars with the chocolate mixture along with the vanilla extract. Gradually integrate the egg mixture, beating continuously. Add the flour and cocoa and mix until completely combined.
  4. Pour the mixture into the prepared mold. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, until it rises but is still slightly soft in the center and when a wooden toothpick is inserted into the center, it comes out with some moist crumbs and looks cracked.
  5. Let it rest in the mold for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack and let it cool completely. Cut into squares.
